## Tips for Better AI Image Prompts

The quality of AI-generated images depends heavily on how you describe what you want. A few principles that consistently produce better results:

- **Be specific about setting.** "A cozy Italian restaurant with candles and brick walls" beats "restaurant interior."
- **Describe lighting explicitly.** "Warm golden-hour light" or "bright, clean studio lighting" tells the AI what mood to create.
- **Include the subject's action or state.** "A barber trimming hair with precision scissors" works better than "barbershop."
- **Mention the visual style you want.** Use the style selectors in AI Studio (photorealistic, editorial, cinematic) to guide the output.
