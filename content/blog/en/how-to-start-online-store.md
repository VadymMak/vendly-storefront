Starting an online store used to mean weeks of work, thousands of euros, and a developer on speed-dial. Not anymore.

With VendShop, small business owners across Slovakia, Germany, and Austria are launching professional websites in under 5 minutes — with AI chatbot, WhatsApp integration, and multilingual support built in from day one.

## Step 1: Choose Your Business Type

VendShop supports 10+ business types: restaurants, beauty salons, repair shops, fashion stores, medical clinics, and more. Each type comes with a pre-configured template optimized for that industry.

Just select your category and the platform adapts the layout, colors, and features to match your needs.

## Step 2: Add Your Business Details

Fill in your business name, address, working hours, and contact info. This takes about 60 seconds. Our AI assistant can even generate professional product descriptions and SEO metadata for you automatically.

## Step 3: Upload Your Products or Services

Add your first product, service, or menu item. Upload photos — our image pipeline automatically crops and optimizes them for web. Set prices, categories, and availability.

> **Pro tip:** Start with your 5–10 best-selling items. You can always add more later.

## Step 4: Go Live

Click "Publish" and your store is live on a `yourname.vendshop.shop` subdomain. Share it on Instagram, WhatsApp, or Google My Business immediately.

If you're on a paid plan, connect your own domain (like `myshop.sk`) in one click.
